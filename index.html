Identity Theft

  • Stolen credit card used for unauthorized purchases.
  • Personal data leaked in major data breaches.
  • Criminals open bank accounts in victim's name.
  • Impersonation on social media for malicious purposes.
  • Tax fraud using stolen Social Security numbers.
  • Snatching Sensitive Info from Your Mailbox.
Cyberbullying

  • Persistent online threats and harassment via email.
  • Unknown person stalking victim on social media.
  • Repeated intimidation through anonymous online messages.
  • Unwanted attention from an online acquaintance turned stalker.
  • Cyberbullying with hurtful messages and rumors circulated.
Malware

  • Phishing email leads to Trojan virus infection.
  • Drive-by download infects user's computer unknowingly.
  • Ransomware encrypts files on an individual's device.
  • Spyware captures sensitive information without user's knowledge.
  • Keylogger records keystrokes to steal login credentials.
Online
Extortion

  • Blackmail threats using compromising images to extort money.
  • Ransom demand issued to website owner to prevent a DDoS attack.
  • Hacker threatens to publish stolen data unless compensated.
  • Cybercriminal demands payment to avoid releasing sensitive information.
  • Extortionist threatens to harm an individual's reputation unless paid.
Child
Exploitation

  • Distribution of explicit photos of minors on social media.
  • Sexual predators targeting minors in online gaming communities.
  • Illegal sharing of explicit content featuring underage individuals.
  • Online grooming by adults seeking inappropriate contact with children.
  • Child pornography ring discovered on the dark web.
Unauthorized
Access

  • Former employee gains unauthorized access to company's confidential files.
  • Hacker breaches email account, sending unauthorized messages.
  • Unauthorized users infiltrate a closed online community to disrupt operations.
  • Phishing attack leads to unauthorized access to university's research database.
  • Intruder gains access to an individual's private cloud storage.
